About Us

Learn more about AldricWeb and our journey

Our Story

AldricWeb was founded in 2025 by Abdulquadri Balogun Adeyanju with a vision to transform how businesses in Nigeria and beyond approach their digital presence.

Starting as a solo freelancer, Abdulquadri quickly built a reputation for delivering high-quality web solutions that combined aesthetic appeal with technical excellence. As demand grew, AldricWeb expanded into a full-service web development company based in Ibadan, Nigeria.

Today, we're proud to have helped numerous businesses establish powerful online identities through our comprehensive web development services.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with innovative web solutions that drive growth, enhance user experience, and establish a strong digital presence in an increasingly connected world.

Our Vision

To become the leading web development company in Africa, known for creating transformative digital experiences that help businesses thrive in the global marketplace.

Our Values

  • Excellence in every project
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Client-centered approach
  • Integrity and transparency
  • Continuous learning and improvement

Meet Our Founder

Abdulquadri Balogun Adeyanju

Founder & CEO

Abdulquadri is a passionate web developer and digital strategist with over 9 years of experience in creating impactful web solutions. With a background in Computer Science and a keen eye for design, he bridges the gap between technical functionality and aesthetic appeal.

His journey began as a self-taught developer, fueled by curiosity and a desire to help local businesses establish their online presence. This passion evolved into AldricWeb, where he leads a team dedicated to delivering exceptional web experiences.

When not coding, Abdulquadri enjoys mentoring aspiring developers and contributing to open-source projects.
